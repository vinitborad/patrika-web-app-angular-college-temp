Our Pricing Philosophy

At Patrika Printers, we believe in transparent, fair, and competitive pricing for all our printing services. Our pricing structure is designed to provide high-quality printing services at affordable rates without any hidden charges.

Volume Discounts

We offer volume discounts for bulk orders as follows:

  • 50-100 pages: 5% discount
  • 101-200 pages: 10% discount
  • 201+ pages: 15% discount

Special Paper Options

Premium paper options are available at an additional cost:

  • Glossy paper: +₹2 per page
  • Card stock: +₹3 per page
  • Recycled paper: +₹1 per page

Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards
  • UPI Payments
  • Net Banking
  • Digital Wallets

Cancellation and Refund Policy

Orders can be cancelled before the printing process begins, and a full refund will be processed. Once printing has started, cancellations and refunds are not possible. If there is a technical issue with our kiosk that prevents you from collecting your prints, please contact our support team for assistance.

Price Changes

We reserve the right to change our prices at any time. Any price changes will be reflected on our website and application and will apply to new orders only. Orders that have already been placed will not be affected by price changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our pricing policy, please contact us at contact@patrikaprinters.com.
